Panel Application

Thank you for your interest in contributing to The Great Burlesque Expo 2014.

GBE is February 7-9, 2014 at The Hyatt Regency Hotel in Cambridge, MA.

GBE is looking to find interesting classes, panels and other convention presentations that satisfy as many different attendees as we can. We are looking for dance classes, circus arts classes, other movement classes, stagecraft class, performing arts business management classes, panels and discussions, and other material that may be of interest to amateur or professional burlesque performer.

We encourage both experienced and new teachers to bid events. Whether it's a new idea or material that's been run before, we're interested in what you have to propose.

The Great Burlesque Expo 2014 actively solicits bids in the months leading up to the Expo. As the attendance grows, based on registration numbers, we may need a additional round of bids to fill in the slate of games already accepted for the convention.

Please fill out the submission form with as much information as possible. This is the best way to help us evaluate your class or panel quickly!

Panel Proposal Instructions

Panels at the Expo are comprised of a collection of notable personalities and experts discussing a topic of interest to the GBE community. Topics can include aspects of the Burlesque business, performance choices, political issues or any other subject that can have an impact on the scene. The best panels are those that get us thinking and talking about how to make the community better.

Submitting a panel idea does not require or imply that you must sit on the panel as a moderator or panelist. If you're interested in sitting on a panel - go to the "Sit on a Panel" page to volunteer for panels that are under current consideration for this year's Expo.

Other Frequently Asked Questions About Classes and Panels

Why Bid Early?

There are several great reasons to get your event bid in early:

  • You're likelier to get the time slot you desire. As the schedule fills, the prime time slots fill up.
  • Your game gets more publicity and advertising. It's likelier to fill earlier, which will give you more time to cast and plan.
  • You get a better chance at attracting the congoers who know that it's smart to sign up early for events.
  • You might miss your chance to bid at all if you wait.
Important Note:
The policy of the The Great Burlesque Expo 2014 has changed this year. This year, time spent assisting the expo during run time as a teacher, panelist, perfomer, or other volunteer will be taken into consideration for 2015 discounts. After the GBE has concluded, you can expect to hear the about 2015 discounts as part of our thank yous. If you are coming to the GBE purely to teach, please inform the teacher coordinator, Scandal from Bohemia. via email and we will arrange a pass for you.
