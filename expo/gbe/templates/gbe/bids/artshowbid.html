Art Show Submission

Please fill out the form completely. The Art Show is a juried exhibit. If your submissions are not considered appropriate for display, you will not receive a refund of the first panel fee. If you opt to register for multiple panels and are not selected, the money for the second panel will be refunded.

Additional panels may be available for $25.00 each. If they are, we will alert the artists whose work has been accepted.
  • - -

  • Panels are 4'x4' pegboard. Limited hanging hardware is available on site. You are responsible for making sure all artwork fits on your panel(s).
